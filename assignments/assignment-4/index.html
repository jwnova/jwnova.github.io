SAVE THE CATS

User Flow Map

Prototype

DOCUMENTATION

This project is an application based on mobile devices. Through this product, users can upload or view the information of cats in their area, so as to timely rescue lost cats, stray cats, cats in danger, they can buy pet supplies for cats in the online store, and can also communicate with other users.

The core function of the product is as a platform for users to exchange information. To sign up for an account, users need to fill in their name, email address, password and location, or log in with a Facebook or Google account. Then users can browse through items that have been updated in the online store, view details such as prices and functions, and choose to add them to their shopping cart or complete payment. In the Map, users can locate their location and see nearby cats who need help or other users.

In Discover, users can browse content posted by other users, they can also edit text and images to post a new content, or search for specific content through the search bar. In Message, users can view their chats with other users and have conversations. In Me, users can view and modify their personal information, platform preference settings and other content.

The product is completely web-based, and it also relies on cloud services because it needs to store data on items in the online store and content posted by users. Since the content needs to be updated in time based on the user's location, the product requires location technology. In addition, online payment technology and search engine technology are also needed. The product can be connected to mobile devices such as phones, tablets and smart wearables.

The design of the product itself does not have many innovative features, it is mainly the integration of existing mature application like online shopping, maps, forums and chat rooms. in a new, niche area, but it did not look like cluttered or complicated.

It focuses on the overall UI architecture, the model should be clear, consistent, recognizable, and bring related things together, separate unrelated things, and make similar things similar to each other. Moreover, to ensure visibility of the application, it should not have redundant information. The design should allow the user to see the options and materials clearly during operation without distraction. Finally, the design should be flexible, allowing users to undo and redo to reduce errors and misuse.

Common information platform are a little less interesting, but combining information communication with specific area can enhance the interactive experience between the tool and the user. In addition, platform applications have a fixed way of profit, they usually relying on advertising, but this application directly includes online stores to meet the needs of profit.
